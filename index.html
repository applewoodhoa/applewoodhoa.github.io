Welcome to the Applewood HOA website.

How do I...

  • report a street light out?
    See Huntsville Utilities Report a Problem Page
  • report a pool issue?
    Contact the board at board@madisonapplewoodhoa.com
  • get a replacement pool card?
    Cards are $50 for each replacement. Contact Elite Housing at (256) 808-8719 or info@ehmusa.com
  • contact the board?
    Please email us at board@madisonapplewoodhoa.com
  • get a recycling bin?
    Go to Recycling Alliance of North Alabama website and request a bin.
  • request an approval for an exterior change?
    Please submit a request via Elite's website.
  • get a garbage can repaired?
    Go to Madison County Waste Control's websiteand request a repair.

Utilities

Electric service is provided by Huntsville Utilities.

Water service is provided by Madison Utilities.

Natural gas is provided by North Alabama Natural Gas District.

Garbage (Household Waste) pickup is provided by Madison County Sanitation on Thursdays.

Recycling pickup is provided by Recycling Alliance of North Alabama. This is new since August 1st 2019. You must have a new style bin which is the same size as your garbage bin. They pickup many things including cardboard boxes, #1, #2 narrow neck bottles. Pickup switched to a monthly cadence on the 3rd Thursday of the month.

Trash pickup is provided by Republic Services on Mondays. This is for yard waste and large items. What is the difference between trash and garbage?

Internet, TV & Phone service is provided by AT&T Fiber. There is no service provided by WOW or Comcast.
